Kelp Shake

Description

Quench your thirst with this iconic Bikini Bottom beverage, straight from the world of SpongeBob SquarePants. The Kelp Shake is a refreshing and nutritious drink that's beloved by residents of the deep blue sea. Follow our simple recipe to whip up this green delight at home and enjoy a taste of underwater charm with every sip. Perfect for fans of all ages, this drink is sure to make a splash!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Soy Milk
  • Handful of Spinach
  • 1 tsp Kelp Extract (Secret Ingredient)
  • 1 Cup of Pineapple
  • 1 Green Apple
  • 1 Banana

Step-by-Step

  1. Add all the ingredients into a blender
  2. Turn the blender on. That was easy!
