Krabby Patty

Description

You've taken a delicious dive into Bikini Bottom's most famous dish. The Krabby Patty is more than just a burger; it's a culinary icon beloved by SpongeBob fans everywhere. Follow our step-by-step recipe to recreate the magic of the Krusty Krab right in your own kitchen. Get ready to enjoy the legendary flavors that have made this burger a hit in the deep blue sea!

Ingredients

  • 1 Beef Patty
  • 1 Burger Bun
  • 1 Slice of Yellow American Cheese
  • 1 Slice of Tomato
  • 1 Slice of Lettuce
  • 1 Slice of Onion
  • 5 Slices of Pickle
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • Vegetable oil and/or butter for frying

Step-by-Step

  1. Season the beef patty with the salt, pepper and MSG. Rub this in thoroughly.
  2. Add the oil or butter to the pan and put it on a medium heat.
  3. Cook both sides of the patty for 2 minutes to get it brown on both sides.
  4. Place the patty on the burger bun and add the lettuce on top
  5. Add the slice of cheese on the lettuce
  6. Add the onions on top of the cheese
  7. Add the slice of tomato on the onions
  8. Spread ketchup and mustard on top of the tomato to personal liking
  9. Finally the absolute most important part- do not forget the pickles!
  10. Close the top bun and there you have it. One Krabby Patty!
